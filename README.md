## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
2. Describe your AI agent idea in the text area
3. Click "Generate Intent"
4. Copy or export your Intent Capsule

## Design Philosophy

Built by a 20-year product veteran who believes AI agents should be designed with product taste, not just technical specs. Intent Capsules bridge the gap between product vision and implementation.
